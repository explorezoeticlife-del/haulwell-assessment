How It Works In The Real World

Built For Schools, Fleets & Drivers

The same ecosystem serves three groups at once — each entering at the point that fits their role, all connected through Driver Readiness™.

🎓

CDL Schools

Prepare students for the real road
  • Add readiness education alongside CDL training
  • Run the Foundations Mini-Series as classroom content
  • Give every graduate a Driver Readiness Card™
  • Track cohorts through the Cohort Dashboard™
🚛

Fleets

Keep drivers healthy and retained
  • Onboard new hires with the Readiness Assessment™
  • Catch early strain through Yellow Zone™ Awareness
  • Offer the 12-Week Program™ for stabilization
  • Monitor fleet-wide readiness on the Dashboard™
🧭

Drivers

Stay steady, mile after mile
  • Know where you stand with the Continuum™
  • Carry the Readiness Card™ as a daily anchor
  • Reach the AI Coach™ anytime, on the road
  • Lean on Code of the Road™ for real situations
